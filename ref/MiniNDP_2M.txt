RAMPAC possible output port addresses to select more than 2 banks

Normal RAMPAC has max 2 banks
To select any bank:
A8  low
A9  high
A10 low=bank0 high=bank1
A15 high

bank_0 block_N = OUT129,n
bank_1 block_N = OUT133,n

1 11111 00
